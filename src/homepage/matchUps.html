Strong Matchups

Genji

Despite Genji's mobility, Hammond can easily track him with primary fire. Your shields neutralize his damage output, and Piledriver can disrupt his positioning after dash.

Widowmaker

Your mobility counters her sniper positioning. Close the gap quickly with Grapple and pressure her with constant harassment. She struggles up close, take advantage of that.

Hanzo

Similar to Widowmaker, Hammond's mobility makes it difficult for Hanzo to land consistent shots. Approach from unexpected angles and disrupt his positioning.

Baptiste

While his Immortality Field can be annoying, you can easily force Baptiste out of it by booping him.

Difficult Matchups

Sombra

Sombra can hack Hammond out of Roll and Grapple, completely stopping his mobility. Watch for when she uses her translocator and coordinate with your team to pressure her out.

Cassidy

His high damage with fan the hammer along with his ability to hinder you make him a rather difficult to deal with

Roadhog

Roadhog's Hook completely kills your momentum and brigs you closer to him where Roadhog excels.

Brigitte

Her Shield Bash and Whip Shot can interrupt your mobility. The burst healing from her Repair Pack reduces your damage output. Try to engage when her cooldowns or shield is down.

Ana

While she's vulnerable to dives alone, her team comp can make up for that. A skilled Ana can Sleep Dart you mid-roll allowing for her team to follow up. Approach with caution.

Junker Queen

Her Commanding Shout provides team-wide durability that can negate your dive impact. Her Jagged Blade can interrupt your momentum and function similarly to hog hook.

Zenyatta

Zenyatta's discord orb forces hammond to play on the defensive since he can easily be killed very quickly if he is not careful.
