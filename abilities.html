Quad Cannons

Wrecking Ball's primary weapon is a set of four automatic cannons that fire rapidly, dealing damage at short to medium range.

Stats:

  • Damage: 5 per bullet (2.5 at long range)
  • Rate of Fire: 25 rounds per second
  • Ammo: 80 rounds
  • Reload Time: 2 seconds
  • Falloff Range: 15-25 meters

Tips:

  • The Quad Cannons are most effective at close range due to their spread pattern.
  • They're excellent for finishing off low-health targets after using your crowd control abilities.
  • When aiming, maintain tracking rather than flicking due to the hitscan nature of his guns.

Roll

Wrecking Ball transforms into a ball, increasing his maximum movement speed and allowing him to roll through enemies, dealing damage on impact.

Stats:

  • Damage: 50 per collision
  • Movement Speed: 10 m/s (150% increase)
  • Cooldown: None (Toggle ability)
  • Knockback: Minor knockback on collision

Tips:

  • Use Roll to quickly traverse the map and return to the fight after respawning.
  • Rolling through enemies can disrupt their positioning, especially snipers and supports.
  • While in Roll form, you cannot use your primary weapon but gain increased mobility.
  • Roll makes you harder to hit due to your smaller hitbox and faster movement speed.
  • Mastering the transition between Roll and normal mode is key to Hammond's playstyle.

Grappling Claw

Wrecking Ball launches a grappling claw, attaching to surfaces and allowing him to swing around the environment, building momentum for powerful strikes.

Stats:

  • Range: 23 meters
  • Cooldown: 5 seconds
  • Max Swing Duration: Infinite until you go into fireball, and then it is 8 seconds

Tips:

  • Grappling Claw is Hammond's signature ability, enabling his unique movement mechanics.
  • Use environmental objects to swing around corners and surprise enemies.
  • The grapple can be released early by pressing the ability button again.
  • When combined with Piledriver, this ability creates is a pretty heavy hitting combo.

Piledriver

Wrecking Ball slams into the ground below, dealing damage and launching enemies into the air, making them vulnerable to follow-up attacks.

Stats:

  • Damage: 20-100 (based on distance from epicenter)
  • Vertical Knockback: 5-20 meters
  • Area of Effect: 8-meter radius
  • Cooldown: 10 seconds
  • Requirements: Must be airborne to activate

Tips:

  • Piledriver is most effective when targeting multiple enemies grouped together.
  • The knockup effect allows for easy follow-up attacks with your Quad Cannons.
  • Use it to interrupt enemy ultimates that require channel time.
  • Combine with Grappling Claw to gain height before executing Piledriver.
  • Can be used as an escape mechanism when in danger, as the animation provides brief protection.
  • Perfect for displacing enemies from high ground positions or off the map entirely.

Adaptive Shield

Wrecking Ball generates temporary personal shields that increase based on the number of enemies nearby, providing crucial survivability.

Stats:

  • Base Shield: 100
  • Additional Shield per Enemy: 100
  • Maximum Shield: 700 (with 6 enemies)
  • Duration: 7 seconds
  • Cooldown: 15 seconds

Tips:

  • Activate Adaptive Shield when diving into multiple enemies for maximum value.
  • The additional health doesn't feed enemy ultimate charge, making it valuable in team fights.
  • Time this ability after using Piledriver to survive focus fire when you're vulnerable.
  • Can be used preemptively before engaging if you expect to take heavy damage.
  • Remember the shield health decays over time, so don't activate it too early.
  • When used properly, this ability makes Hammond one of the tankiest heroes in the game.

Minefield (Ultimate)

Wrecking Ball deploys a field of proximity mines that damage and knock back enemies. Perfect for area denial and zoning during team fights.

Stats:

  • Damage per Mine: 130
  • Activation Time: 0.5 seconds
  • Duration: 20 seconds
  • Knockback: Significant vertical and horizontal
  • Ultimate Cost: 1850 points

Tips:

  • Deploy Minefield from above for maximum spread and effectiveness.
  • Combine with Piledriver to deploy mines while enemies are airborne, making them land on the mines.
  • Use to control objectives or deny areas during critical pushes.
  • Mines can be placed on payload or moving objectives for mobile area denial.
  • Effective at splitting enemy teams when placed between frontline and backline.
  • Can be used defensively to protect your team during retreats.
  • Mines can be destroyed by enemy fire, so place them strategically.
  • Minefield is unique in that it is one of the few ultimates in the game where you can build towards you next ult while they're deployed

Perks

Wrecking Ball can unlock perks as he levels up. Minor perks become available at level 2 and major perks at level 3. These allow him to enhance specific aspects of his kit and survivability.

Steamroller (Minor Perk)

  • Type: Minor Perk
  • Effect: Roll impacts deal 100% more damage to Tanks
  • Damage: 120 to Tanks
  • Headshot: ✕

Upsides:

  • Highly effective against tank-heavy comps, giving Hammond more burst potential.
  • Rewards aggressive dive play with stronger openings in team fights.

Pack Rat (Minor Perk)

  • Type: Minor Perk
  • Effect: Health packs heal an additional 100 HP

Upsides:

  • Enhances self-sustain when playing independently or away from healers.
  • Synergizes well with Hammond’s mobility, allowing fast rotations to health packs mid-combat.
  • Works on both Mini and Mega health packs.

Hang Time (Major Perk)

  • Type: Major Perk
  • Damage: 150 - 20
  • Duration: 1.1s windup
  • Movement Speed (up): 3.7 m/s
  • Movement Speed (side): 6 m/s

Upsides:

  • Massively increases burst potential when comboed with grapple setups.
  • Air control during windup allows creative use of map geometry.

Transfer Efficiency (Major Perk)

  • Type: Major Perk
  • Effect: Adaptive Shield transfer costs 50% less health
  • Cooldown Reduction: -1.5s per ally affected

Upsides:

  • Improves team utility by allowing safer, more frequent shield sharing.
  • Pairs well with clustered team fights and brawls where allies are grouped.
